[ My Midnight Blue ]

Scientific Name:

Midnight Blue

Discovered by / at:

Edith Macclane 2036

Family:

Marine

Habitat:

Deep and dark in the ocean

Appearance:

A jellyfish with its tentacles waving and moisture flowing inside the body. It would gradually change its color as it float.

Key Characteristics:

  • Floats in the sea.
  • Foodie
  • Vulnerable to noises

What we know so far about [ Midnight Blue ]? Midnight blue is an adorable jellyfish-like creature living in the deep sea. Generally, it shows a friendly attitude towards human interaction. But it is frighrtened of noises and would lose some abilities if there is noise.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Press the mouse to give food to midnight blue. It would show a happy face and approach the food. However, when the key is pressed you give the habitat a noise. Midnight blue would feel afraid and shiver. After the noise, midnight blue wouldn't be able to freely move again.
